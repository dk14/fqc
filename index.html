⚡️ Why we are skeptical about quantum computing

The author of this project believes that the hype surrounding quantum computers vastly exceeds their practical, near‑term capabilities. A detailed critique can be read at q.doomsdayexplorer.online .

In short: the qubits we currently can build are noisy, expensive and nowhere near the scale required for the algorithms that are often advertised. Relying on “quantum supremacy” for real‑world quantitative research is, at best, a gamble.
